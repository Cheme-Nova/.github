## What ChemeNova Does

Specialty chemical R&D has not changed its methodology in decades. Formulation is still done by hand — trial, error, institutional memory, and luck.

We are replacing that with **IntelliForm™**: an agentic AI platform that takes a product specification in natural language and outputs optimized, constraint-validated formulations aligned to cost, sustainability, and regulatory targets.

This is not a chatbot for chemists. It is an autonomous decision engine that understands chemistry.

---

## The Platform

### IntelliForm™ — Agentic Formulation Intelligence

The core platform. A chemist describes a target product; IntelliForm reasons across material constraints, green chemistry metrics, regulatory regions, and cost posture — and returns a ranked set of formulation candidates.

**Open-source community shell** → [Cheme-Nova/IntelliForm](https://github.com/Cheme-Nova/IntelliForm)

```
Natural Language Spec
        ↓
Constraint Graph Builder
        ↓
Multi-Objective Optimizer  ←─── RDKit · chemprop · GAUCHE
        ↓
Ranked Formulation Candidates
        ↓
Green Chemistry Audit + Regulatory Check
```

### PrintChem™ — Smart Ink & Coating Formulation

Functional ink and coating optimization for advanced printing applications. Phase: results validation.

### OrbitChem™ — Industrial Process Intelligence

Real-time process analytics for chemical hardware. Physics-Informed Neural Networks + FFT vibration analysis transforming centrifuge and reactor data into self-optimizing assets.

**Prototype** → [Cheme-Nova/ai-node](https://github.com/Cheme-Nova/ai-node)

## Design Principles

**Domain-first, not feature-first.** Every capability we build starts with a real formulation problem, not a technology looking for a use case.

**Autonomous, not assistive.** The goal is systems that generate and validate — not systems that wait for a human to type the next prompt.

**Science-grounded.** Green chemistry metrics and circular sourcing are embedded in the optimization logic, not bolted on as a report at the end.

**Open where it creates value.** The community IntelliForm layer accelerates the field. The enterprise core is where proprietary constraint libraries and client IP live.
